First Steps

Just start typing away!

Don't worry: You can't break anything!

You'll know (or at least recognize) most buttons and/ore mechanisms in this editor from other applications. So just keep going and create your page as you would do using your text processor (like Microsoft Word). Some mechanisms will work a little differently though (e.g. working with images and the little to no possibilities to alter the font size or font colour).

IMPORTANT: SAVE OFTEN
